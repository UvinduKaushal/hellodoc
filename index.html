About HelloDoc

Founded in 2024, HelloDoc is Sri Lanka's leading digital healthcare platform, revolutionizing how patients access medical care and how healthcare providers deliver services. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional healthcare and modern technology, making quality healthcare accessible to everyone across the island.

Our Comprehensive Healthcare Ecosystem

  • AI-Powered Medical Assistant: 24/7 intelligent health guidance with 95% accuracy rate
  • Telemedicine Platform: Secure video consultations with board-certified physicians
  • Digital Pharmacy: Licensed medication delivery with same-day service in major cities
  • Healthcare Marketplace: Verified medical products from trusted suppliers
  • Community Support: Donation portal helping over 10,000 patients annually

With partnerships across 15+ hospitals and 200+ pharmacies nationwide, HelloDoc serves over 50,000 active users monthly. We're committed to maintaining the highest standards of medical ethics, data security, and patient privacy while delivering exceptional healthcare experiences.
