Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our healthcare services

How do I book an appointment with a doctor?

+

You can book an appointment through our online platform by visiting the appointments page. Simply select your preferred doctor, choose an available time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

What services does HelloDoc offer?

+

HelloDoc offers a comprehensive range of healthcare services including doctor consultations, AI-powered health assistance, medicine ordering, and donation programs. All services are designed to make healthcare accessible and convenient.

Is my personal health information secure?

+

Yes, we take your privacy and security very seriously. All personal health information is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with healthcare privacy regulations and never share your information without your explicit consent.

How does the AI health assistant work?

+

Our AI health assistant uses advanced machine learning to provide health-related information and guidance. It can answer general health questions, provide symptom information, and help you understand medical terms. However, it's not a replacement for professional medical advice.

Can I order medicine online?

+

Yes, you can order medicine through our platform. Simply browse our medicine catalog, select the items you need, and complete your order. We ensure all medicines are genuine and properly stored. Prescription medicines require a valid prescription from a licensed doctor.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

How can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

+

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment through your dashboard or by contacting our support team. Please note that cancellations should be made at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment.

Do you offer emergency medical services?

+

HelloDoc is designed for non-emergency healthcare services. In case of a medical emergency, please call your local emergency services immediately or visit the nearest emergency room.

How do I contact customer support?

+

You can contact our customer support team through our contact page, email us directly, or use our live chat feature. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or concerns.

Are your doctors licensed and qualified?

+

Yes, all doctors on our platform are licensed medical professionals with verified credentials. We thoroughly vet all healthcare providers to ensure they meet our high standards of care and professionalism.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, our support team is here to help.
